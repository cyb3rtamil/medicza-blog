Why This Exam Matters

The TELC certification is recognized globally and is a mandatory requirement for work visas (Ausbildung, Nursing, IT jobs) and university admissions in Germany. Clearing this exam opens the door to the European Union.

Our curriculum conquers the four pillars of the TELC format:

  • Hören (Listening): decoding rapid native speech.
  • Lesen (Reading): understanding complex texts and articles.
  • Schreiben (Writing): drafting professional emails and essays.
  • Sprechen (Speaking): engaging in spontaneous discussions.
